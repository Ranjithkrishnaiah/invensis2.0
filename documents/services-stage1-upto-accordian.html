Manual Software Testing Services

Businesses are placing great importance on cost optimization and allocating an increased budget for quality assurance and software testing. Gauging the quality of software is necessary before pushing it into the production pipeline. Manual testing provides a reliable and accurate way of detecting flaws in a software application.

High quality and cost-effective manual testing of applications, devices, and wearables is critical for ensuring the usability and functionality of the application. Manual testing is cheaper, more likely to detect real user issues and is flexible. However, it can be time-consuming and may require testers that are more skilled.

Invensis Technologies is an experienced Software Testing Company, who will provide you with the right solutions for your manual testing requirements. At Invensis, we have qualified and experienced testers to assist you on your specific projects. We have considerable experience in completing manual testing projects successfully leading to a satisfactory end-user experience.

Invensis offers specialized manual testing services that extend across different aspects of the software development cycle. The manual testing is carried out either on a stand-alone basis or as a precursor to automated testing.
