Invensis Technologies Sponsors 2016 CSCB National Annual Conference

Dec-11-2019 Views Share

Associates itself with the conference for sixth year in a row as a commitment to furthering the interests of the Canadian customs brokerage industry.

Bangalore, India, September 24, 2016– Invensis Technologies, a prominent IT-BPO service provider, will be attending the 2016 CSCB National Annual Conference. The conference will be held from September 25-27 at Queen’s Landing, Niagara-on-the-Lake, Ontario, Canada.

The CSCB National Annual Conference is regarded as the most significant gathering of the customs broker industry. The attendees include the who’s who of companies, specialists and associate members who are active contributors to the industry. It is organized by the Canadian Society of Customs Brokers.

The 2016 National Annual Conference is based on the overarching theme: ‘Tools of the International Trade: The Value of Data, from Visioning to Versioning '. A host of interesting topics pertinent to the Customs Brokerage industry would be covered during the three-day conference that includes:

Canada in the Global Economy

Beyond the Big Border

CBSA’s Commercial Vision and CARM: An Update on Plans and Priorities

CBSA’s Commercial Projects: Simultaneous sessions

Big Data-Don’t Drown in It- Profit From It

Digital Customs

Customs Brokers of North America: From Suchiate to Poker Creek

Using Social Media as a business tool: Opportunities for SMEs

With over 16 years of expertise in delivering end-to-end customs brokerage support services, Invensis Technologies has established a strong reputation in the Canadian customs brokerage market. Association with the 2016 CSCB National Annual Conference for the sixth successive year in a row reflects Invensis' confidence in the importance of the conference to further the interests of the Canadian customs brokerage industry. We at Invensis consistently look for opportunities to provide value to our customers. Our endeavor has led to a new offering - Big Data & Analytics services. The value to our customers in the customs brokerage industry is in using the actionable insights obtained through our analytics service to identify new channels of growth and profitability. We have analytics solutions for customer acquisition, customer lifetime value, revenue forecasting, manpower capacity planning, industry research, supply chain analytics, new customer listing, competitor analysis and financial analytics to name a few.

Meet us at the 2016 CSCB Conference in Ontario, to find out more about our services and how we can transform your business.

For more information about the conference, please visit cscb.ca.

For more information about Invensis Technologies' customs brokerage services, please visit www.invensis.net.

About Invensis Technologies

Invensis Technologies is an IT-BPO service provider with offices in Wilmington, DE; and Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Rajahmundry in India. Since 2000, the company has been catering to the diverse business needs of clients across a range of industries, including manufacturing, automotive, hospitality, customs brokerage, healthcare, energy, banking, e-commerce and retail. The company provides outsourcing services to enable organizations worldwide to improve process efficiencies and enhance business performance.

ABOUT CSCB

The Canadian Society of Customs Brokers (CSCB) is among the most authoritative and regarded organizations in Canada's international trade community. Representing the interests of Canada's customs brokers, the Society actively seeks and achieves improvements in government policies and procedure, provides advice and guidance to Canadian and international regulators on new policies, and builds strong partnerships in the industry to create effective business solutions. The Society now has a membership of over 160 customs brokers, 4,100 CCS (Certified Customs Specialist) designates, 200 CTCS (Certified Trade Compliance Specialist) designates, and 600 students on an annual basis.
