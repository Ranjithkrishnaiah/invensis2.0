Invensis Young Thinker Scholarship Essay Award

At Invensis, we believe innovative ideas can come from any professional in our organization and are not just the prerogative of managers. Extending this philosophy, we would like to encourage young thinkers to continue on the path of success and earn recognition that would enable them to get a foothold in the industry of their choice.

It is our pleasure to announce the launch of the 'Invensis Young Thinker Scholarship Essay Award'. This award is a unique opportunity for students in USA, UK and Australia to showcase their knowledge and skills in the form of an insightful essay and win laurels for their efforts.

The scholarship prize for the best essay received across all regions is USD 500. The award-winner and the best essay will also be showcased on the Invensis website, blog and social media. Winning this scholarship award will not only entitle you to a monetary prize, but will also be an added merit on your resume for future job applications.

Details about the award are given below. Please read through each point and the terms and conditions before submitting your essay.

About Contest

  • Topic: "How Robotic Process Automation is shaping the future of Business Process Outsourcing?"
  • Word count: 600 words or more
  • Last date for submission: Nov 30, 11.59pm

Eligibility

  • The 'Invensis Young Thinker Scholarship Essay Award' is open to all undergraduate students who are currently enrolled in universities in USA, UK and Australia.
  • Applicants must be within the age group of 18-26 years.
  • Citizenship of the country is not mandatory.
  • Candidates must have a minimum cumulative GPA score of 3.0.

Selection

  • The award will be given on the basis of an Essay Writing Contest on a topic decided on by Invensis.
  • The award winner will be chosen on the basis of originality, research, writing skills and creativity shown in the essay.
  • The award winner will be selected by a panel of qualified judges instituted by Invensis.

Essay Guidelines

  • The essay should be written only in English.
  • The essay should be completely original in its content and not copied from any source.
  • Referring to credible sources or publications is allowed and hyperlinks must be provided to these sources.
  • The essay must be substantiated through research, data, expert view points and / or graphs as cited in reference materials.
  • The essay must be properly formatted, with headline, sub-headings, paragraph breaks, bullet points and alignments (as required). Correct usage of punctuation and grammar is expected
  • Please include your name, address and contact details in the essay document.

Submission Guidelines

  • Please provide correct contact information in the submission form
  • To submit the completed essay, please fill in the submission form on this page.
  • The essay must be uploaded in Word (.doc) / PDF format in the submission form.
  • Please provide a photo of yourself (less than 500 KB in size) along with the essay.
  • The essay must be properly formatted, with headline, sub-headings, paragraph breaks, bullet points and alignments (as required). Correct usage of punctuation and grammar is expected
  • Please include your name, address and contact details in the essay document.

Scholarship Award Prize Declaration and Dispatch:

  • The award winner will be selected on or about Dec 20, 2016.
  • The award winner will be contacted thereafter on the contact information provided in the form.
  • Eligibility criteria will be verified. The award winner will be asked to furnish:
    • Statement or transcript of your Grade Point Average (GPA) - unofficial transcripts accepted.
    • Scan of your college ID card or admission proof.
  • The check will be dispatched to the confirmed address of the winner.
  • The award winner and the winning article will be showcased on our website and / or blog.
